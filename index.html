About Me

A Curious Engineer on a Mission

My journey into software engineering began with a simple question: "How does this work?" That curiosity has never left me. From writing my first lines of HTML to building multi-functional web applications, every step has been driven by a genuine love for creating things that matter.

I believe that great software is not just about clean code — it's about solving real human problems. Whether it's a business tool that saves hours of manual work, a website that helps a small business grow, or an application that makes someone's day a little easier, I build with purpose.

I'm continuously growing my skill set, exploring new technologies, and working to become a technology leader who can make a meaningful impact. My approach combines technical precision with creative problem-solving — because the best solutions require both.
